## Badge Rule

Do not redesign any faction badge. Wallfoot hybrids / 墙下混裔 do not have a formal state emblem, national crest, official badge, sacred seal, gang logo, or new faction logo. Their visual identity is object-based and survival-based:

- grey-blue short jacket and market cloth
- terracotta inner layer
- old yellow cloth lining
- black-market wooden pass tag
- broken pottery charm
- hidden pocket stitching
- narrow hidden knife
- tiny dark-green scale arcs behind the ear
- torn flag cord or traded cloth only as a material trace, not as a badge

The board must not create a new Lu Qingli personal crest or a new Wallfoot hybrid emblem. Existing Zhaoming, Suming/Qingming, and Northern emblem assets must not be copied into her clothing, background, or prop board. If old imperial flag cloth appears, it should be a torn traded scrap, partially buried by cord or pocket stitching, never a clean emblem display.

## Character Identity Lock

Lu Qingli is a 15-16-year-old Wallfoot hybrid girl and early New Blood figure. She is not a thief archetype, not a monster girl, and not a formal rebel-uniform wearer. She survives by reading exits, trading objects, hiding tools, and staying close to walls. Her smile is a test rather than intimacy.

Core visual anchors:

- petite 15-16-year-old body, fast and compact rather than decorative
- back-near-wall body language
- wary sideways smile and calculating eyes
- grey-blue short jacket
- terracotta and old-yellow inner layers
- narrow belt and rope knots
- hidden pocket construction
- black-market wooden pass tag
- broken pottery charm
- narrow hidden knife
- tiny dark-green scale arcs behind one ear, visible only in close detail
- sleeve-edge kiln dust and clean functional repairs

## State Notes

### Wallfoot Market Outfit

This is Lu Qingli's default identity state around Wallfoot market, kiln caves, and black-market trading. The outfit is compact, complete, and used hard, but not ragged. Grey-blue short jacket and terracotta inner cloth dominate. Trade objects may hang in controlled clusters, but she should look ready to hide or detach them quickly.

### Escape Light Outfit

This state reduces visible hang-points and tightens the silhouette. Add deep grey short wrap or shoulder cloth, pull tokens inward, keep the narrow hidden knife and black wood pass accessible. The character should read as someone who may betray, run, or return to cut a mark rope.

### Borrowed Snowline Outfit

This state borrows border survival pieces without turning Lu Qingli into a Grey Wall soldier. Use borrowed old scarf, short leather knee guards, snow dust, and frost wear. The grey-blue short jacket, hidden pockets, black wood pass, and pottery charm remain the identity lock.
