## Character Identity Lock

Bai Yi is an upper-intelligence insectoid ruler form serving as the Suming central inspector and Qingming Register executor. His caste has already become extremely human-like in facial shape. He must not be an ugly insect head, skull mask, beak, mandible face, or white chitin face-plate monster. The face should read as a cold, handsome, near-human high official: long refined face, narrow jaw, high bridge, thin lips, clean bone-white skin or a very thin insect-wax glaze. The insect traits that remain are concentrated in highly readable dark compound eyes, a height above 2 meters, a very slender vertical body, elongated hands, restrained joint language, a mandatory small money-tail / rat-tail pigtail at the back of the bald head, and a translucent wing-membrane shoulder cape.

The compound eyes must be stronger than ordinary dark eyes. Each eye should be a large almond-shaped black obsidian compound-eye surface set into a human eye position. There should be no sclera, iris, or human pupil. The surface should show dense micro-lens dots, honeycomb-like grain, wet black lacquer highlights, and granular reflections. The eye shape may be slightly larger and more elongated than human eyes, but it must preserve facial beauty: clean eyelid rims, refined brow line, high nose bridge, narrow jaw, and thin lips.

Core visual anchors:

- tall vertical stillness
- over 2 meters tall and unusually slender
- high cylindrical Qingming official hat integrated with head carapace
- one thin low back money-tail / rat-tail pigtail hanging from the bald occiput or from under the rear edge of the official hat
- dustless insect-wax white official robe
- bone-white hard collar
- near-human cold handsome face with bone-white insect-wax complexion
- large almond-shaped black compound eyes in normal human eye positions, with visible micro-lens dots and wet obsidian highlights
- translucent wing-membrane shoulder cape
- thin white straps or bead chains hanging from the hat and back collar
- small locked Qingming black-sun/white-wing institutional mark
- cold-gold pins and wax fasteners
- Qingming white register
- insect-wax needle
- cold-white Qingming incense thread

## State Notes

### Qingming Inspector Official Robe

This is Bai Yi's default inspection and village-screening state. The silhouette is clean, over 2 meters tall, high, still, and bureaucratic. The robe is long and white, the collar is hard and bone-like, the face is cold and near-human rather than monstrous, and the small institutional mark is present as a controlled authority cue. White register and wax needle must be readable as tools, not magical props.

### Pseudo-Human Ruler Formal Robe

This state is used for public office, official declaration, and institutional performance. The outfit may appear most human-official in outline: high hat, long robe, vertical front, hanging straps, and ceremonial stillness. The non-human truth must remain visible in the large black compound eyes, abnormal height, very slender body, neck membrane, subtly elongated hands, and the single thin pigtail at the back of the bald head. Do not show full human hair, ordinary human pupils, sclera, or iris.

### Pursuit Official Robe

This state narrows the robe and folds the wing cape for travel and tracking. The figure still carries a white official silhouette rather than combat armor. Qingming incense is a thin cold-white tracking line that clings to cloth and pauses; it is not an attack spell or smoke weapon.
