## Character Identity Lock

Luo Qinghe is a 37-year-old Canyang Ao village physician, Shen Weisang's mother, and a quiet keeper of old relay-road code. She must read as a warm, beautiful, resilient adult woman, not an old woman, priestess, court noble, witch, or battlefield heroine. Coarse village clothing should not erase her beauty: her face is refined and calm, eyes gentle but decisive, skin warmed by medicine-room smoke and outdoor labor, posture stable and protective.

Core visual anchors:

- blue-green cloth head scarf wrapping practical work hair
- herb-green apron over grey-white or earth-brown medicine-room top
- narrow sleeves for grasping herbs and moving in a tight medicine room
- herb basket and medicine packets
- waist-hung small copper scissors, herb thread, cloth pouch, and simple cutting knife or needle case
- clean but worked fabric with herb stains, smoke dust, and small repairs
- old wood medicine cabinet, herbs, mortar, water jar, and concealed cabinet-bottom old-relay mark
- half Sun-Moon relay token only as hidden/pass-off object, never a clean public emblem
- smoke-dark fire-state distress that preserves dignity and mother/physician identity

## State Notes

### Medicine-Room Daily State

Use Canyang Ao human-life colors: earth brown, herb green, old wood brown, blue-green cloth, late-wheat warmth, and low smoke grey. The silhouette should be practical and readable as a village physician: apron, head scarf, herb basket, narrow sleeves, and waist tools. Clothing can be old and stained, but remains clean, complete, fitted, and professional.

### Old-Relay Code State

Keep the same medicine-room disguise. The old relay identity is communicated through behavior and hidden objects: hand blocking a cabinet bottom, herb names used as code, a narrow old-pattern waist tape mostly under the apron, a small key/tool kit, and the half-token almost covered in the palm or cloth. The visual must not reveal a clean badge or turn her into a secret-order priestess.

### Fire Farewell State

This is the episode-02 farewell state. Keep mother and physician dignity. Add smoke, soot, loosened scarf, singed apron edge, small shoulder-side wound indication, and a hand passing the half-token. Do not make the image bloody, collapsed, ragged, or melodramatic. The costume stays identifiable as the same Luo Qinghe.
