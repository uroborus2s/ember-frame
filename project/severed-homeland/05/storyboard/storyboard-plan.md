## 1. 基础设定 / Basic Setup

中文：本分镜来自 `05/script/final-script.md`，不改剧情，只把场面拆成可绘制、可生成、可回查的横屏镜头。

## 2. 氛围和画质 / Atmosphere and Image Quality

### 2.1 风格核心 / Style Core

中文：超写实、电影级质感、低魔东方史诗、16:9横屏宽屏剧；真实重心、真实速度、自然呼吸、接触反作用和可读运动路径。
English: hyper-realistic cinematic low-magic Eastern epic, 16:9 landscape widescreen drama; real body weight, real speed, natural breathing, contact reaction and readable movement paths.

### 2.2 视觉基调 / Visual Tone

中文：制度空间洁净压迫，旧驿空间裂纹残光，边墙/北境空间寒冷狭窄，生活空间先有秩序再被破坏。
English: Institutional spaces feel clean and oppressive, old relay spaces carry cracked residual light, border/northern spaces are cold and narrow, and living spaces must show order before damage.

### 2.3 色彩和影调 / Color and Tonal Range

中文：按场景 bible 使用土褐/草药青、深河青/蒸汽白、虫蜡白/黑日、黑石/雪白、月白/金赤等分区色彩。
English: Follow the scene bible color zones: earth-brown and herb green, deep river teal and steam white, insect-wax white and black sun, black stone and snow white, moon-white and gold-red.
