# Episode 03 Art Direction - Targeted SC003-SH001 Run

## Scope

This targeted art-room pass covers only `SC003-SH001`, a 16:9 video-generation reference frame for the ruined old-well mouth. It does not mark the full Episode 03 art package complete.

## Direction Lock

- Keep the scene grounded in fire-aftermath realism: charred wood, controlled smoke, ash on old white stone, grass rope, old well stone, and partial warm firelight.
- Preserve the episode continuity hierarchy: Shen Weisang and Yan Nanzhi are the only current foreground/midground living protagonists; Luo Qinghe is inherited through the herb-ash-covered wall marks, not as a living figure in the present action.
- Make the old-well geography readable before beauty: well mouth, white stone wall, low hidden-door seam, well frame, pressure direction, and escape route must be clear at thumbnail size.
- Treat the pursuing slave soldier as pressure in the background, not a monster showcase. The low rear queue, hybrid posture, wrist-binding/net arrest function, and wax-grey-green uniform cues should distinguish him from pure insect soldiers and officials.
- Use firelight and ash to lower contrast without swallowing character silhouettes.
