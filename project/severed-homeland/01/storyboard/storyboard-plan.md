# 第01集《白册进村》Storyboard Plan / Bilingual Storyboard Plan

## 1. 基础设定 / Basic Setup

中文：本 storyboard 按 35 个稳定镜头 ID 编排，目标成片时长约 4 分 30 秒，16:9 横屏宽屏剧。分镜只根据当前重制后的 `01/script/final-script.md`、角色/场景 bible、连续性报告和脚本评分生成；旧版资产和旧版分镜只作为目录格式参考，不作为剧情 canon。

English: This storyboard uses 35 stable shot IDs for an approximately four-and-a-half-minute 16:9 landscape widescreen drama. It is based on the current remade `01/script/final-script.md`, the character and scene bibles, continuity report and script score. Legacy assets and storyboard files are used only as structural references, not story canon.

## 2. 氛围和画质 / Atmosphere and Image Quality

### 2.1 风格核心 / Style Core

中文：超写实、电影级质感、真实触感古代材质、低魔东方史诗。动作必须有真实重心、真实速度、自然呼吸、接触反作用和清楚运动路径；禁止漂浮慢动作、僵硬站桩、塑料 CG、网红脸和无来源灾民群像。

English: Hyper-realistic cinematic quality, tactile period materials and grounded low-magic Eastern epic. Movement must have real weight, real speed, natural breathing, contact reaction and readable motion paths; avoid floaty slow motion, stiff posing, plastic CG, influencer faces and source-less refugee crowds.

### 2.2 视觉基调 / Visual Tone

中文：本集的恐惧来自制度洁净和生活门槛被越过。清明香、白册、虫蜡针、白蜡旗、旧井、药柜暗格和猎弓弦是主要视觉按钮。旧帝国痕迹必须带裂纹、尘灰和代价，不做纯净圣物。

English: Fear comes from institutional cleanliness crossing domestic thresholds. Qingming incense, white register, insect-wax needle, white wax flags, old well, medicine cabinet compartment and bowstring are the main visual buttons. Old imperial traces must carry cracks, dust and cost, never pure sacred glamour.

### 2.3 色彩和影调 / Color and Tonal Range

中文：SC001 使用黑石、雪白、冰青、旧铁和暗红；SC002 使用麦金、河泥褐、湿木灰和虫蜡白；SC003 使用潮湿暖暗、朱赤红线、月白冷反光；SC004-SC006 使用残阳坳土褐、草药青、旧木褐与肃明虫蜡白的冲突。

English: SC001 uses blackstone, snow white, ice blue, old iron and dark red; SC002 uses wheat gold, river mud brown, wet wood grey and insect-wax white; SC003 uses humid warm darkness, vermilion red cord and moon-white cold reflection; SC004-SC006 contrast Canyangao earth brown, herb green and old wood with Suming insect-wax white.
