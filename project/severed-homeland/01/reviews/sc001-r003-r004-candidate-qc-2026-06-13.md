## Latest Feedback Read

User feedback corrected three constraints:

- A gatehouse view facing outside must not show houses, city streets, warm windows, city towers, or a city backplate. The outside direction is the siege field only.
- The bell is not a complete decorative bone bell. It is a five-thousand-year-old Zhaoming old-empire metal alarm bell: oxidized, dented, cracked, repaired, with chipped rim and only broken unreadable fragments of the old sun-moon astrolabe emblem.
- The fallen soldier in R003 is not dead. He is alive, knocked down by the gate-impact aftershock, bracing in snow mud while the bell swings.

2026-06-14 follow-up feedback added three stricter inheritance checks:

- Exterior attackers must be `C020` northern siege troops plus `C021` companion beasts, with `E01_C020` episode siege state. The outside field cannot read as a human army, generic single-species orc horde, or ownerless monster pack.
- The bell emblem is the global `P016` Zhaoming sun-moon astrolabe remnant. `P018` Northern Beast Alliance marks are allowed only on attacker-side shields, hide banners, tack, or siege gear, never on the bell.
- The R003 soldier must inherit `E01_C024A` and read as alive after aftershock: half-open or squinting eyes, visible breath, bracing hand, bent knee, slipping boot. Closed corpse eyes or a limp dead face fail QC.

The storyboard micro-edit description is recorded in `01/storyboard/sc001-r003-r004-microedit-script-2026-06-13.md`.
